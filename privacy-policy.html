Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This privacy policy explains how the mobile application Islamic Kids: Quiz & Games (“the App”) handles user data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, locations or any other identifying data.

2. No Internet Tracking

The App does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or cookies. All gameplay happens on the device itself.

3. No Third-Party Ads

The App does not display third-party advertisements. There are no ad networks integrated into the App.

4. No In-App Purchases

The App contains no in-app purchases or payment processing of any kind.

5. Child-Friendly Design

The App is designed for children and families and aims to comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and applicable child privacy regulations. The content is educational and age-appropriate.

6. Permissions

The App does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage, beyond what is required by the operating system to install and run the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated “Last updated” date.
