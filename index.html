Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/01/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application Islamic Kids Games (the “App”).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This App is designed primarily for children and families.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos or videos

The App does not require users to create an account or log in.

2. Children’s Privacy

This App is intended for children and families. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that personal information has been provided unintentionally, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

3. App Functionality

The App includes:

  • Islamic educational quizzes
  • Prophet-themed quizzes
  • Memory games
  • Coloring and drawing activities

All game progress and drawings are stored locally on the user’s device only.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases using Google Play Billing to unlock additional content such as quiz levels or content packs.

All payments are securely processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information such as credit card details.

Purchases are optional, and the App can be used without making any payment.

5. Advertisements

The App does not display third-party advertisements.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Google services:

  • Google Play Billing (for in-app purchases)

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App and its content. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the risk to user privacy is minimal.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
